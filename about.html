Our Philosophy

Sustainable Built Environments (SBE) was formed in 2001 by a multi-disciplinary team of industry leaders passionate about the role that the built environment can have in shaping thriving communities and cities.

Since the first project we completed, our holistic and integrative approach to design has become our philosophy. As architects and engineers we understand the importance of inter-disciplinary collaboration to the successful delivery of a performing building. As dedicated ESD consultants our aim is to work with and support design teams navigate the sustainable building landscape – from meeting Town Planning and statutory energy efficiency requirements to designing high performing and innovative buildings, and achieving best practice voluntary certifications.

In a period characterised by increasing global, national and local energy efficiency and sustainability objectives our knowledge and understanding of policies and regulations, building physics, and resource efficiency strategies allows us to translate a project’s requirements into functional and integrated design outcomes.
