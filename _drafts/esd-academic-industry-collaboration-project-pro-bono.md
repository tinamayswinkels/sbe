I’ve started working on a real retrofit project for an 18-year-old Art Building at Melbourne High School, with mindset guidance and tech support from SBE. <br /><br />After I learned how to use Better Building (formerly Speckel), I’ve been developing a calibrated base model to generate the building’s energy performance, and testing a range of retrofit initiatives: Lighting, glazing, daylight, building fabric, renewable energy.<br /><br />A few things I’ve taken away from the process so far:<br />\- Always come back to the core project goals<br />\- Prioritise strategies based on justified decisions<br />\- Translating client needs into workflows is a big part of the process<br /><br />The project is still in progress, right now I’m deep in the evidence based analysis, working through a bunch of energy data to identify opportunities (probably the most fun part!).<br /><br />I am really grateful for the mentorship of SBE , bouncing around ESD ideas each week. It’s been incredibly valuable for someone like me who is genuinely drawn to the process of measuring sustainability in the built environment.<br />
